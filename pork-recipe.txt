Ingredients:
 - 2 tbsp salt
 - 1/2  ginger
 - 1/2 tsp back pepper
 - 2 full rack of back ribs
 
Directions:
 1. Slice the reibs to smaller pieces
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the salt, pepper and ginger. 

 3. Rube the salt, ginger and pepper on the ribs and let it reset
    for at least 1 hour

 4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Grease a large square baking pans.

 5. Bake 80 to 95 minutes or until the meeat is tender. 

 6. Wait till the ribs are cooler and serve it.
